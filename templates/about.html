Purpose Of Our App

Our web app is designed and created by 3 students who are junior developers in MMU to help freshies and our juniors who just enrolled in MMU to break the ice and interact with each other as it will develop and maintain long-lasting friendships and connections in this university during the period of the WIN week of MMU every new intake that happens.That is our top priority and aim for us three.

Features That We Included In The Web App

1. Private Messaging System: This feature allows MMU students to have a one-to-one or private conversation with each other about random stuff that they wish to which can help them to get to know each other more, learn about their interests, and build lasting friendships.

2. Forum Boards: This feature allows MMU students to voice their opinion out or post their comments publicly on the forum boards which in return can help MMU students to discuss about the WIN week and share their own experiences with other random students.

3. Search Bar: This feature allows students to search for other students' profiles that are already registered in the database which will display their profile picture and bio.

4. Login/Logout: This feature allows students to login and logout from the web app.

5. Profile Picture/Bio: Allows MMU students to upload their own profile picture of their choice and add their bio.

6. QR Code And Achievement Record: MMU students can scan other students' QR Codes to add them as a fellow friend and put them in a friend list and the achievement record tracks the number of friends that they made during the period of the win week.
